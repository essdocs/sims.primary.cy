Simple awarding of points to pupils.
{:.lead}

> You may see customised labels for ‘Achievement’ or ‘Behaviour’. Your school administrator can change the labelling on the **Conduct Configuration** screen. E.g., Merits and Demerits, or Awards and Sanctions.
{: .note}

## Awarding achievement points

One of the nice ways of interacting with the Class Log is selecting one or more pupils and be able to award them achievement points.

1. Open the {% include glossarytooltip.html explain="Menu" %}.
1. Use the {% include glossarytooltip.html explain="Menu Search" %} to find the {% include glossarytooltip.html explain="Class Log" page=page %}.
1. Choose your class.
1. Select one or more pupils.
1. Click **Record Achievement** {% include icon.html url="face-happy" title="Achievements" %} on the toolbar.

> Your school administrator can configure what needs recording for each achievement option on the **Conduct Configuration** screen.
{:.note}

## Recording behaviour incidents

If there're children who you're less pleased with, you can quickly record a behaviour incident.

1. Open the {% include glossarytooltip.html explain="Menu" %}.
2. Use the {% include glossarytooltip.html explain="Menu Search" %} to find the {% include glossarytooltip.html explain="Class Log" page=page %}.
3. Choose your class.
4. Select one or more pupils.
5. Click **Record Behaviour** {% include icon.html url="face-sad" title="Behaviour" %} on the toolbar.

> Your school administrator can configure what needs recording for each behaviour incident option on the **Conduct Configuration** screen.
{:.note}
